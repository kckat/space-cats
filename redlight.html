makin it RED

  • navigate on to: General > Accessibility > Display Accommodations > Color Filters
  • by now you've found yourself at this screen on the right with the switch off. flip that bad boy on.
  • scroll down to where it says Color Tint and click it so that row is checked
  • down on the sliders slide the intensity slider all the way up and the hue slider all the way to either end and that will make it red
  • don't PANIC everything is fine.

makin it easy to switch on and off

  • scroll back to the Accessibility screen
  • scroll to the very bottom and hit Accessibility Shortcut
  • click color filters.
  • triple click your home buttom and it should switch between red and normal
